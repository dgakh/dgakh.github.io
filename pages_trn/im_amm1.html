Administrative Mastery & Management

The course is aimed for those who wants to improve his/her professionalism, technical and human/people skills, and the abilities to guide the efforts of others: to plan, organise, direct, motivate, co-ordinate and control their activities so that the desired objectives of the department or enterprise are achieved in the most efficient and economical manner.

  1. Practical psychological knowledge to improve workplace interactions
  2. Practical administration action tools
  3. Manage changing roles and responsibilities whether working with bosses, peers, team members or customers
  4. Meet dynamic work expectations by expanding proactive capabilities
  5. Clearly and confidently communicate and negotiate to manage conflicts and achieve results
  6. Apply emotional intelligence and effective listening practices to job
  7. Use strategic diplomacy to handle office politics, difficult people and demanding situations
  8. Overcome and exceed work demands
  9. Understand administrative problem solving
  10. Manage office politics
  11. Action tools for office management
  12. Discover personality type for better human relations
  13. Improve memory techniques
  14. Master human relations as administrative reform tool
  15. Project professional credibility, authority and presence
  16. Communicate assertively and achieve the intended impact
  17. Plan, organize and control workload and priorities
  18. Build and gain the authority needed to get the job done
  19. Create and maintain key inter-relationship models to access information and support
  20. Analyze situations, make decisions and solve problems amicably
  21. Help to improve the working life of team
  22. Raise performance from merely activity level to leadership

Lecturer: Dr. Ibrahim Mammadzadeh
