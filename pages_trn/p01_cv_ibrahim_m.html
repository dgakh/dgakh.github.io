Ibrahim Museib Mammadzadeh

Ibrahim Mammadzadeh is an experienced business leader and professional economist for the strategic planning, business development and international consulting. He is also an experienced leader of multi-disciplinary teams dealing with important international projects. He currently fellow of the EU’s Institutional Development Team and is a leading specialist in Sector/Industry Reforming, Restructuring and Privatization. Ibrahim has direct experience at all levels of the legal, political, technical and financial aspects of the preparation, adjudication and negotiation of capacity building and HRD schemes. His professional experience has been augmented by periods of secondment and employment with major international agencies, including World Bank and United Nations dealing with macro aspects of project planning, appraisal and implementation. He has experience in international contracting; fully experienced with FIDIC Contract Conditions. He has held a number of business and professional posts and visiting academic appointments in the Azerbaijan and overseas and is the author of a number of technical and business management papers. Ibrahim is regularly invited to draw on his wide operational experience and give advice and seminars to International Funding Agencies. He is author of 5 books and 95 technical papers.
