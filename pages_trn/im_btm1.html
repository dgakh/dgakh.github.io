Business Taxation Management

This cource ourlines management of finances for payment of tax, assessing the advance tax liability to pay tax in time. Tax management has nothing to do with planning to save tax it is just related with operational aspect of payment of tax i.e. while managing its taxes an organization ensures that it is making timely payment of taxes without running out of the money and he is complying with all the provisions of the law.

  1. Understanding the concepts of a Value Added Tax in principle
  2. Recognizing the impact of VAT on your business in practice
  3. Designing & understanding VAT Accounting System
  4. Learning to how to implement VAT for your business, including setting up accounting systems and billing
  5. Understanding international VAT transactions
  6. Understanding VAT and e-commerce
  7. Understanding how the GCC Framework will interact with the EU VAT system and other VAT systems
  8. Generate accurate VAT returns & reduce VAT penalties for errors
  9. Gain practical knowledge how to implement VAT without errors
