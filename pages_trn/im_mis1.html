Management Information Systems

This course explains how Inforamtion Technologies can help to manage business of an organuzation better. With ever increasing complexities of business as well as management, now a days, Information Systems are not merely required for improving efficiency of business operations; rather these systems are used for effective business decisions and achieving strategic advantage.

  1. Data collection concept
  2. Data gathering and leveling
  3. Data exchange for project communication and coordination
  4. Information flow for quality assurance
  5. Database management
  6. Information spreading and accumulation
  7. Data composition and decomposition
  8. Information resources planning
  9. Information for organogram designing
  10. Information and decision-making process

Lecturer: Dr. Ibrahim Mammadzadeh
