Motivation

Confining work leads to lack of body movement and poses a threat to the health. Wushu is one of the best way to preserve and improve the health by spending small ammount of time. Proposed course requires from you about 25 minutes of excercises and small space. Thus it can be performed within limited office space. The course is based on exercises of "Ба Дуань Цзин" ("8 кусков парчи") but improved to better mastering and have better effect. The complex "Ба Дуань Цзин" was created by ancient chinese general for purpose of preserving health of the troops in campaign.

Base Course

Base course consists only of simple exercises for upper part of the body. (See Russian Page for details.)

Full course

Full course includes exercises for upper part of the body and for leags. exercises for legs require from a physically undeveloped individual lot of time to master. But full course provides health for all body, especially for abdominal organs. (See Russian Page for details.)
